# Seeded Patient Scenarios

The seed data is designed to tell small clinical stories instead of looking like random placeholder rows.

## Elena Garcia

- MRN: `MRN-000001`
- Scenario: acute office visit for sore throat and fever
- Key points:
  - severe penicillin allergy
  - diagnosed with acute pharyngitis
  - treated with azithromycin
  - complete blood count ordered
  - elevated WBC result suggests active infection
  - follow-up appointment is scheduled

Why this case matters:

- shows encounter workflow
- shows medication history
- shows allergy relevance to prescribing
- shows lab ordering and result review

## Michael Johnson

- MRN: `MRN-000002`
- Scenario: chronic disease follow-up
- Key points:
  - existing hypertension
  - type 2 diabetes without complications
  - active lisinopril and metformin
  - HbA1c result remains above goal

Why this case matters:

- shows ongoing conditions rather than a one-time acute visit
- shows active medication queries
- shows chronic lab monitoring

## Priya Nair

- MRN: `MRN-000003`
- Scenario: annual wellness exam
- Key points:
  - routine preventive visit
  - mild latex allergy
  - lipid panel ordered
  - elevated LDL result captured

Why this case matters:

- shows preventive care workflow
- gives the dataset variety beyond illness follow-up
- supports abnormal lab reporting examples

## Why These Stories Help the Project

Using a few coherent patient scenarios makes the project easier to understand and demo. A reviewer can quickly see how appointments, encounters, medications, allergies, and labs connect through realistic workflows.
